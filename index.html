Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the beginning of each new game.

To play, use the four crystal buttons below to match the random number you have been given. Each crystal will add a random specific amount of points between 1-12 to your score.

You win the game by exactly matching your score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Pay close attention to your score as you click each crystal, the point value they are assigned is hidden from you until you click each button.

Each time the game is restarted, the game will change the value of the random number and each crystal.
